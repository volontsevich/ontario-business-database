FAQ

Questions people usually ask first.

Is payment required today?

No. This page is for early access validation. The planned price is C$15, but no payment is collected today.

What format will I receive?

The planned format is CSV, which can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Airtable, and most import tools.

Will it include all Ontario businesses?

The goal is broad Ontario coverage, but the first release may focus on the most requested cities and categories.

Does it include direct emails?

Only public contact details will be considered, and only where legally and practically appropriate. Contact pages may be included instead of direct emails.

When will it be ready?

The release date depends on demand. Early access submissions help determine whether to build the first downloadable version.
