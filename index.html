FAQ

Questions people usually ask first.

How is the database delivered?

The database is delivered as a CSV file that works in Excel, Google Sheets, Airtable, and most import tools.

What is included in the file?

The file includes business name, city, category, website, public contact details where available, source URL, and last verified date.

Will it include all Ontario businesses?

The goal is broad Ontario coverage across cities and business categories.

Does it include direct emails?

Only public contact details will be considered, and only where legally and practically appropriate. Contact pages may be included instead of direct emails.

What can I use it for?

Common use cases include market research, prospecting, outreach planning, local business analysis, and agency sales workflows.
